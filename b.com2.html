Education of Commerce:-

The full form of BCom is Bachelor of Commerce. BCom is a three-year UG degree course recognised by the Universities Grants Commission (UGC). Students can pursue B.Com programmes as full-time BCom, Distance BCom, or Online BCom. BCom courses are one of the most popular courses amongst the Class 12 students with the Commerce stream. According to a survey report, with nearly 9.33 lakh enrolment, BCom has been the third most desired course in India after Class 12.
