Streaming Commerce (Live Commerce):

Benefits of Streaming Commerce: Real-time Interaction: Streaming commerce allows for real-time interaction between sellers and buyers. This live interaction can enhance customer engagement and provide an opportunity for immediate feedback and questions. Product Demonstration: Sellers can showcase products or services in action through live demonstrations, giving customers a better understanding of the features and benefits. Trust Building: Live interactions and demonstrations can contribute to building trust between sellers and buyers, as customers get to see the product or service in real-time. Limited-Time Offers: Streaming commerce often involves limited-time promotions or special offers, creating a sense of urgency and encouraging immediate purchases. Social Sharing: Viewers can share live streams on social media, potentially increasing the reach and visibility of products or services. Personalized Experience: Sellers can address individual customer queries and provide a more personalized shopping experience, similar to an in-store interaction.
