Bachelor of Science (BSc.) in Nursing :-

A Bachelor of Science (BSc.) in Nursing is a comprehensive undergraduate degree program that prepares students for a rewarding and vital career in healthcare. This program combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on clinical experience to develop competent and compassionate nurses who are equipped to provide quality care to individuals, families, and communities. Throughout the program, students learn about anatomy, physiology, pharmacology, nursing theory, patient care, and healthcare ethics. They also gain practical skills through supervised clinical rotations in various healthcare settings.
